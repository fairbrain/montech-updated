ABOUT US

Montech Entertainment Private Limited has been engaging the younger generation as a pioneer in producing unique animation material. Over the years, our characters have become incredibly popular, contributing to the company's rise to prominence in the fields of licensing and merchandise, movie production and distribution, digital business, retail stores, and events. We apply our profound creative knowledge to all tasks, completing projects of any size or complexity. We aim to create lively animations by focusing on even the tiniest details, as they can have a significant effect.

Montech Entertainment Private Limited encourages businesses everywhere to create their most recognizable pieces. In order to deliver the universal art of storytelling to audiences worldwide, our award-winning teams of artists and engineers collaborate with the national and international creative community in the fields of cinema, television, animation, gaming, brand experience, and advertising. We offer top-notch2D and 3D animations, modelling, multi-award-winning visual effects (VFX) and gaming services to the world's media, advertising, and entertainment sectors by fusing creativity and technology. Nothing is too big for us to produce to an exceptionally high caliber.

Our Work

Montech Entertainment Private Limited has contributed to upskill the constructive future in the animation world with creativity and originality. We collaborate with both national and international filmmakers to create animated tales that are remarkable. We are in a field that is always changing, revolutionizing advertising and brand experiences with a potent blend of artistry and narrative. Rapidly adjusting to a changing media environment, we are maximizing the possibilities of novel and developing technologies.

Montech Entertainment Private Limited has worked on around 15 projects with Reliance Animation Studio Private Limited, including the project ‘Bhaiya ji Balban’. Along with this, we have also engaged with several international projects like ‘Titoo’, ‘Dabangg’ and ‘Lambu G, Tingu G’. All of these projects were made in collaboration with the Singaporean company ‘Cosmos Maya’. Also, we are currently working with the renowned animation company ‘Graphiti’ on the project ‘Bharat Hai Hum’ (KTB) Other notable workpieces made by us are ‘Creepy Candy’, ‘Courtroom’, ‘Chikoo aur Banty’ and ‘Narayana’ with ‘Animatzione’ and ‘Fukrey Boyzzz’ in collaboration with ‘Paperboat’. There is another project that we are working on at the moment which is called ‘Jay Jagannath’.

Currently, we are working on the project ‘Bireshwar Vivekananda’ partnering with the Ramakrishna Mission, funded by Ministry of Culture, Government of India. ‘The Missile Man’, ‘Tota Kahini’ and ‘Juto Abishkar’ are our own IP made by our production team.Our main objective is to introduce our Indian artworks to the Western world, and also inculcate the use of advanced technology from the West.
